About This Project

WatchaScore is a free, open-source football scoreboard overlay designed for streamers and amateur broadcasters. Customize team names, scores, match events, and the clock in real-time.

I am continuously working to improve WatchaScore. Your feedback and suggestions are highly valued!

If you find this tool useful, please consider supporting its development. Every contribution helps keep the project alive and growing.
